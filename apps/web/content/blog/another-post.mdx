Tab started from a simple frustration: splitting a fourth expense with roommates shouldn't require a subscription.

## The problem

Most expense apps follow the same playbook. Free tier with artificial limits. Daily caps. Wait timers. Dark patterns that make you feel guilty for wanting basic functionality.

## Our approach

Tab is different. Create as many tabs as you want. Add as many friends as you need. Settle up when you're ready. No paywalls, no surprises.

We're open source. Your data, your control. If the product changes in a way you don't like, the code is there.
